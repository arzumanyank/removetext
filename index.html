Privacy Policy for Remove Text App

This Privacy Policy governs the manner in which Remove Text collects, uses, maintains, and discloses information collected from users of the Remove Text mobile application.

Information Collection and Use

Information We Collect

  1. Text Detection Data: Remove Text utilizes the Vision framework to detect text within images. This data is processed locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.
  2. Machine Learning Data: Our app employs machine learning models to remove text from images. This process also occurs locally on your device, and no data is shared with us.
  3. Advertising Data: Remove Text integrates AdMob advertising to provide ad-supported content. AdMob may collect information for personalized advertising purposes. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for more information on how AdMob collects and uses data.
  4. In-App Purchases: Users have the option to make in-app purchases to access premium features or remove advertisements. These transactions are processed through the respective app store (e.g., Apple App Store, Google Play Store), and we do not collect or store any payment information.

How We Use Collected Information

Collected information is used for the following purposes:

  • Text Detection and Removal: The text detection and removal functionalities are used solely within the app to provide the intended service of removing text from images.
  • Ad Serving: Collected data related to advertising may be used to personalize the ads served to users within the app.
  • In-App Purchases: Information related to in-app purchases is used to process transactions and provide access to premium features or ad-free experiences.

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent users' personal identification information to others. Collected data may be shared with third-party service providers only to the extent necessary to provide the services offered by Remove Text (e.g., ad serving, analytics). These third parties are obligated to maintain the confidentiality of the information and are prohibited from using it for any other purpose.

Data Retention

We retain collected information for the period necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure and reliable.
